End of Life Doula

Guidance on the final journey of life

“The end of life doula role is both new and ancient - a return to how humans have always naturally cared for each other during life’s most sacred event.”

Just as doulas are present at birth, end of life doulas provide specialized services as a person approaches their transition into death. Death and dying are a part of life that can oftentimes cause fear, anxiety or denial. I believe that embracing death as a natural part of life, and planning for it in a thoughtful and meaningful way is essential to living and dying well as it is integral to our wholeness as humans. Through kindness, respect, and professionalism, my role as an end of life doula is to work with dying people as well as their families, to provide comfort through complex emotions as well as mental and spiritual experiences. La Loba Soul Support helps individuals, families, + the culture at large meet the ongoing process of birth, growth, death, + rebirth in a more soul-centric + healing way. In honoring my clients’ wishes, I strive to bring light to the miracle of a life—even in its final stages. I work with community organizations, such as local hospices, hospitals, and religious organizations in growing and deepening the care for the aging and the dying.

“Our grief is a holy thing. It is that proof that another life touched ours in a profound way. It is the mark of love, the mark of connection, and the mark of a life well lived.”

Tracie Barrett-Wesler

Sacred Services

Facing mortality is difficult. Many people feel lost preparing for their own death or the death of a loved one. La Loba Soul Support is here to help, at whatever stage of the journey you may find yourself in. I am here to listen and support you in some of life's most precious and difficult moments with practical, emotional, spiritual support, and most importantly, with a heart-centered human presence.

  • Navigate information and choices around palliative care, hospice, and caregiving for end of life planning
  • Support with facilitating important conversations with loved ones
  • Provide pre-vigil visits to provide companionship, reiki, non-medical care and compassionate touch and personalized guided imagery and meditation to offer soothing, preparative transition practice
  • Develop a personal vigil plan for the final days
  • Explore suggestions and intentions for funeral or memorial service that reflects your loved one’s personality, lifestyle, character and values and facilitates emotional and spiritual healing

  • Life review that deepens understanding of your life and legacy and brings meaning and awareness to your life’s impact
  • Legacy project that is reflective of your life for your loved ones to remember you by
  • Spiritual processing around facing death and what resonates on a personal level that connects you to that which is greater than us
  • Grief support to work through all stages of grief

  • Bedside care provides respite for caregivers, deep listening, companionship, reiki, non-medical care and compassionate touch and meditation
  • Assistance with initiating the vigil plan to bring in a peaceful energy and inner clarity throughout an emotional and chaotic time
  • Vigil support and guidance when death is imminent

  • Grief support for companionship, conversation and / or ceremony planning that reflects your loved one’s personality, lifestyle, character and values
  • Legacy project implementation (on clients behalf)

Packages

La Loba Soul Support provides a full spectrum of services to honor patients at the end of their lives. Packages are tailored to each client based on where that person is in the dying process, their individual wishes, and the amount of support needed by family and caregivers. Whatever package fits a person’s needs, I am committed to them through the death and into the loved ones’ early grief. Please decide for yourselves what works best.

The Standard

This is a full-spectrum package that offers support and guidance during all four phases of the end of life journey.

  • Pre-vigil Visits (totaling 8 hours)
  • Creation of a Remembrance Project
  • Development of a Vigil Plan
  • Emotional, Psychological, & Informational Support
  • 17 Hours of Vigil Presence & Guidance
  • Assistance with Practical Vigil Care
  • Respite for Caregivers
  • Extended Presence at the Time of Death & Afterwards
  • 2 Post Death Visits (totaling 3 hours)

$2,600

  • Emotional, Psychological, & Informational Support
  • 22 Hours of Vigil Support & Guidance
  • Assistance with Practical Vigil Care

  • Extended Presence at the Time of Death & Afterwards
